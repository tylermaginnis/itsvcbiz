Home Computer & Device Help

Friendly, patient technology help right in your Louisville home. No jargon, no stress - just straightforward help when you need it.

What We Help With At Home

Computer Problems

Is your computer running slow? Acting strange? Won't connect to the internet? We can help fix all sorts of computer problems and get things running smoothly again.

  • Speed up slow computers
  • Fix internet connection issues
  • Remove viruses and pop-ups
  • Data backup and recovery

Phones & Tablets

New to smartphones or tablets? We love helping folks get comfortable with their devices. Learn to text, video chat, download apps, and more!

  • Set up new phones and tablets
  • Teach video calling (FaceTime, Zoom)
  • Help with texting and photos
  • Install helpful apps

Internet & WiFi

Having trouble getting online? We can help set up your home WiFi, troubleshoot connection problems, and make sure your internet is working reliably.

  • Set up home WiFi
  • Fix WiFi dead spots
  • Connect devices to internet
  • Improve WiFi signal strength

Smart TVs & Streaming

Want to watch your favorite shows on Netflix, Hulu, or other streaming services? We'll get your smart TV set up and show you how to use it.

  • Set up Smart TVs
  • Install Netflix, Hulu, Disney+
  • Connect to streaming services
  • Show you how to navigate

Common Questions From Homeowners

How much does home help cost?

We offer free estimates! We'll tell you exactly what the cost will be before we do any work. No surprises, no hidden fees.

Do I need to bring my computer somewhere?

Nope! We come right to your home. Stay comfortable while we help you. We'll work on whatever device you're having trouble with.

What if I have questions later?

We're always happy to answer questions, even after we're done helping you. Just give us a call!

Is my information safe?

Absolutely! Your privacy is very important to us. We never share your personal information and always handle your devices with care.
