Quick Tips You Can Use Today

Restart Your Device Weekly

Just like you, your devices need a little rest sometimes! Turning your computer or phone off and back on once a week helps it run better.

Keep Your Software Updated

Those update pop-ups are important! They fix problems and keep your devices secure. When you see an update available, it's best to do it.

Use Strong Passwords

A good password has:

  • At least 8 characters
  • A mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
  • Something memorable to you, not personal info

Back Up Your Photos

Your photos are precious! Make sure to back them up to the cloud or an external drive. That way you won't lose them if something happens to your phone.
